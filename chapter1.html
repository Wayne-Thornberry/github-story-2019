Jimmy's Story


Chapter 1 - A Cold Welcome

It was a warm Monday morning, the birds were chirping, the sun was shining as bright as ever and there was a sweet smell coming from the kitchen. Jimmy is laying in his bed as cosy as ever until his mother called to him for food.
"Jimmy, breakfast is ready" shouted his mom.
Jimmy woke up slowly but shot his eyes open after he remembered what day this is. "its Easter break" Jimmy thought to himself. Jimmy loved Easter break, not just because of the amount of chocolate he can eat. But because jimmy didn't have to go to school. Jimmy didn't dislike school, he just didn't like how much work the school gives him every day. But today was not one of those days, today Jimmy was a freeman.


Jimmy jumped up out of his bed with excitement, he opened his bedroom door and ran down the stairs. He ran through the kitchen door and stopped. Jimmy's eyes widened his jaw dropped to the floor. Jimmy just saw the most shocking thing that would turn his happy Easter break into a nightmare, it was Vicky. His older sister....
